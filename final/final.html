Introduction

This website aims to introduce the viewer to Formula One, a 'motorsport' long known for its world class racing and car engineering. Formula 1 is a very special form of competition because it is one of few which allows engineers, mechanics, and researchers to be part of the team in the quest of building a fast and reliable car that can perform for the drivers who then try to win races. This of course pushes the teams to work hard and develop faster cars and technology, something which I find inspiring.
