The 2019 Season

This page aims to update the newcomer on the current Formula 1 season by introducing the teams and their standings as well as the races of the 2019 season, which at the time of writing was halfway finished. There is also a survey at the end.

Teams

There are ten teams in the 2019 Formula 1 Season. Each team has two drivers aswell as reserve/test drivers.

Mercedes AMG Petronas F1 Team

  • Based in Germany
  • Driver 1: Lewis Hamilton - United Kingdom
  • Driver 2: Valtteri Bottas - Finland
  • Website: mercedesamgf1.com

Scuderia Ferrari

  • Based in Italy
  • Driver 1: Sebastian Vettel - Germany
  • Driver 2: Charles Leclerc - Monaco
  • Website: formula1.ferrari.com

Red Bull Racing

  • Based in Austria
  • Driver 1: Max Verstappen - The Netherlands
  • Driver 2: Pierre Gasly - France
  • Website: redbullracing.redbull.com

McLaren F1 Team

  • Based in the United Kingdom
  • Driver 1: Carlos Sainz Jr. - Spain
  • Driver 2: Lando Norris - United Kingdom
  • Website: mclaren.com

Renault F1 Team

  • Based in France
  • Driver 1: Nico Hulkenberg - Germany
  • Driver 2: Daniel Ricciardo - Australia
  • Website: renaultsport.com

Alfa Romeo Racing

  • Based in Switzerland
  • Driver 1: Kimi Raikkonen - Finland
  • Driver 2: Antonio Giovinazzi - Australia
  • Website: sauber-group.com

Racing Point F1 Team

  • Based in the United Kingdom
  • Driver 1: Sergio Perez - Mexico
  • Driver 2: Lance Stroll - Canada
  • Website: racingpoint.com

Toro Rosso

  • Based in Italy
  • Driver 1: Daniil Kvyat - Russia
  • Driver 2: Alexander Albon - Thailand
  • Website: scuderiatororosso.redbull.com

Haas F1 Team

  • Based in the USA
  • Driver 1: Kevin Magnussen - Denmark
  • Driver 2: Romain Grosjean - France
  • Website: haasf1team.com

Williams Racing

  • Based in the United Kingdom
  • Driver 1: George Russell - United Kingdom
  • Driver 2: Robert Kubica - Poland
  • Website: williamsf1.com
